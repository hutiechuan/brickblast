Brick Blast 8-Bit — Support

A retro 8-bit brick breaker.

How to Play

  • Tap the title screen to start.
  • Drag anywhere to aim, release to fire your balls.
  • Break every numbered brick before the wall reaches the bottom.
  • Collect +1 orbs to add more balls to your next shot.
  • Tap during a shot to fast-forward. Tap the speaker icon to mute.

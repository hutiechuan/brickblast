Privacy Policy

Brick Blast 8-Bit · Last updated: June 13, 2026

Brick Blast 8-Bit (“the App”) is designed to respect your privacy. This policy explains what the App does and does not do with your information.

Information We Collect

None. The App does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. There are no accounts, no logins, and no analytics.

Data Stored on Your Device

Your game progress, such as your best score, stays on your device only. It is never uploaded anywhere.

Network & Third Parties

The App works fully offline. It contains no advertising, no third-party SDKs, and no tracking technologies. It does not connect to any server.

Children’s Privacy

The App is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new date.
